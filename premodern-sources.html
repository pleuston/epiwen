All premodern sources

A register of premodern Chinese epigraphy works (金石學) — the collections, catalogues, and gazetteers that record and study stone inscriptions. The spine is the corpus of 石刻史料新編 (SKSLXB, 新文豐 reprint) as catalogued in Kuhn & Stahl 1991; the SKSLXB location column gives each work's place in that reprint (series.volume:page). The register is not limited to SKSLXB — it also carries the broader epigraphy work canon. Each row opens the work's own page. Browse also by epigrapher 撰者, the inscription concordance, or the 石刻史料新編 contents.
